About me

My name is Amal Janabayev and originally I am from Kazakhstan. I have moved to US when I was 17 to attend a small college in Illinois for which I was swimming competitively. Couple years later I transfered to Indiana Univeristy and focused on education. I picked Economics as a major and graduated in 2018. A little after my graduation I packed my car, said goodbye to all my friends and drove across the country to Seattle. My life in Seattle was a groundbreaking experience which led me to the Tech world.


My pofessional experience includes a variety of fields. I have tried myself in different roles and, quite honestly, enjoyed all of them. My personality is driven by task oriented results and hence the positions I have been filling required just that. Two years of working as a personal trainer and almost a year as a Real Estate Agent is where one would find the highlights of my career path. Tech world is fascinating to me and has many milestones that excite me the more I learn about them.
